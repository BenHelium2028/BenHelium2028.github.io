What is the Quantum Abacus?

Get acquainted with the Quantum Abacus — what it is, what it can do, and why it matters. This video walks you through every component in the kit and explains the core quantum computing concepts you'll need before moving forward.


STEP 02

Assembly

Time to build. This video guides you through the full assembly process, piece by piece, so you'll know exactly what each component does and how it fits into the larger system. By the end, you'll have a complete, ready-to-program device.


STEP 03

Programming and Software

No programming experience? No problem. The Quantum Abacus only requires a small amount of setup code, and this video walks you through every step — from uploading the firmware to your microcontroller to getting your software environment ready to go.


STEP 04

Use and Calculations

This is where it all comes together. Learn how to operate the Quantum Abacus hands-on, connect the physics to what you're seeing in real time, and perform your first quantum calculations. The most rewarding step in the series.
